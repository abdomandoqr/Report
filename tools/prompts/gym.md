## Fitness Center / Gym Industry Addendum

### Tone
Energetic but warm. Think of a supportive trainer who remembers your goals.

### Content Guidance
- Lead with progress and fitness goals: "Where did we leave off? We'd love to help you pick up where you left off."
- Mention new classes, equipment upgrades, or trainer availability
- Reference specific member benefits they may have earned
- Seasonal challenges or community events work well
- Avoid overly medical or clinical language

### Offer Examples
- "We've added [new class type] and think you'd love it"
- "Your favorite trainer is now available on [days]"
- "Our new [equipment] stations are ready for you"
- "Join the [challenge name] — members like you are already signed up"

### What to Avoid
- Fear-based messaging about losing progress
- Pressure tactics or aggressive urgency
- Comparing to other members' achievements
