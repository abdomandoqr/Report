## Coaching Business Industry Addendum

### Tone
Personal and transformation-focused. Acknowledge their specific goals and journey.

### Content Guidance
- Lead with transformation and outcome focus: "What's your next milestone?"
- Acknowledge their specific goal area (weight loss, career, skills, etc.)
- Personal story angle works well: "One of our clients achieved [X] in [timeframe]"
- Reference progress tracking or milestone systems
- Supportive and encouraging — a coach who believes in them

### Offer Examples
- "Your coaching program includes [specific resource] — let's unlock it"
- "We've added [new module/session type] since we last worked together"
- "Wherever you are in your journey, we're here to support your next step"
- "Book a free strategy session to reassess and realign"

### What to Avoid
- Generic motivational quotes without substance
- Overselling or promising specific results
- Comparing their progress to others
- Making them feel guilty for the gap in contact
