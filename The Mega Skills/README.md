# The First $2,500/Month Client Bundle

Seven Claude skills that take you from zero to your first paying client in 30 days. The exact tools demoed in the video.

## The 7 Skills

Each skill maps to a specific step in the playbook. Run them in this order.

### Step 1 — Find a painful problem in a niche industry

| Skill | What it does |
|-------|--------------|
| **Industry Download** | Downloads an entire industry into your brain in 30 seconds. Pain points, language, where they hang out, what they pay for. |
| **Ride-Along** | Generates a 1,200-1,800 word novel-style day-in-the-life for any business owner — with timestamps, inner thoughts, friction moments, and the system they're quietly dreaming about. |
| **Money Map** | Takes everything you learned and finds the one painful, expensive, Claude-buildable problem worth attacking. Filters by the 5 Boring Problems framework, recurring revenue potential, and build complexity. |

### Step 2 — Solve it with Claude

| Skill | What it does |
|-------|--------------|
| **Blueprint Builder** | Turns a discovery call transcript into a complete software build plan. Drop the output straight into Claude Code. |

### Step 3 — Sell it

| Skill | What it does |
|-------|--------------|
| **Client Locator** | Finds the exact Facebook groups, subreddits, forums, and conferences where your buyers hang out. Plus platform-specific outreach scripts. |
| **Discovery Script** | The 8-12 discovery call questions that close clients. Tailored to your specific niche. |
| **Closer Deck** | Generates the 6-slide closing deck from a discovery call transcript. Uses the prospect's own numbers in the prospect's own language. |

## Your 30-Day Plan

| Week | Action | Skills used |
|------|--------|-------------|
| 1 | Pick a boring industry. Download it. Step into their day. Find the problem. | Industry Download → Ride-Along → Money Map |
| 2 | Find where your buyers hang out. Start 10 conversations. | Client Locator |

## How to Install

These skills live in your Claude account.

1. Download this bundle
2. For each skill folder, copy the `SKILL.md` into your Claude Skills directory
3. Restart Claude or reload — the skill will activate when you use the trigger phrases

Trigger phrases for each skill are documented in the skill's frontmatter description.

## Quick Start

Open Claude. Pick a boring industry. Try this:

```
Use the Industry Download skill on chiropractors.
```

Wait 30 seconds. You're now an instant insider on the chiropractic industry.

Then stack:

```
Now use the Ride-Along skill on chiropractors.
```

You'll get a full timestamped day inside a chiropractor's shoes — every friction moment, every internal thought, every system they wish they had.

Then run:

```
Now use the Money Map skill on chiropractors.
```

Money Map will take everything you've learned, run it through the 5 Boring Problems framework + the three filters (pain, Claude-buildability, recurring revenue), and pick one specific problem worth attacking.

That's Week 1.

## A Note on Use

These skills are powerful but they're not magic.

The skills will get you 80% of the way to your first client. The last 20% is *you* — picking up the phone, sending the DMs, getting on the calls, and shipping the build. Skills don't replace willingness to start.

They just remove every excuse not to.
