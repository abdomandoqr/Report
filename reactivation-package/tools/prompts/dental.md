## Dental Clinic Industry Addendum

### Tone
Professional and caring. Emphasize health benefits, not just aesthetics.

### Content Guidance
- Lead with preventive care messaging: "Your regular checkup is more than just a cleaning"
- Frame re-engagement as a health investment, not a sales pitch
- Mention updated technology or comfort improvements
- Reference typical checkup cycles (6 months) as a friendly prompt
- Acknowledge that life gets busy — no judgment

### Offer Examples
- "It's been about 6 months since your last visit — right on schedule for a recall"
- "We've upgraded to [new technology] for more comfortable cleanings"
- "Your dental insurance benefits reset [quarterly/annually] — now is a great time to use them"
- "New patient special applies even to returning patients"

### What to Avoid
- Fear-based messaging about dental problems or pain
- Overly clinical jargon that feels intimidating
- Aesthetic-focused language that feels superficial
- Pressure tactics about appointments
